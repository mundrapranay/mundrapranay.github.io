About Me

I'm a senior at the University of Washington, pursuing a B.Sc. in Mathematics. After graduation in June 2021, I will join the Department of Computer Science at the University of Rochester as a Ph.D. Student. During my undergraduate studies at the University of Washington, I have worked on various software engineering projects, ranging from research papers to personal projects. My main focus areas are Database Systems and Machine Learning. One of my main projects has been Maimon, a system which comes up with acyclic schemas for a give relation. The paper for the project was accepted for the International Database Conference - SIGMOD 2020. I have also worked on Visual Database Management Systems, namely LightDB, where I mapped a high-level Python API to the low-level contracts (i) without sacrificing performance, (ii) minimizing device transfer, and (iii) enabling query expression to a wider audience.
